Poem 3: Colorful Lives

Above the small town
on an unfamiliar planet,
flying.
Out of sight,
out of mind
of those humans,
small h.
Never seen a bird before,
no graceful fliers such as I.
A small child
looks up
at me,
wondering what
the crow in the sky is,
what I mean.
She runs off, looking
for her teacher,
my teammate,
a Human,
capital H.
Flying onward,
seeing the sights,
finding the team leader,
Avrana Kern.
Landing, reporting back,
being proud of myself
for doing good things,
awarded with nothing.
And yet I am happy,
flying over the familiar small town
on an unfamiliar world,
landing in the streets at night.

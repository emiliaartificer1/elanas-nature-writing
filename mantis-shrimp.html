Poem 2: Adventurous

I see the world
for all its beauty
and horror,
brightness
and darkness,
motion
and stillness.
I see others of
my kind
brawling
on the seafloor.
Itching to join,
to beat them
with one strong
strike of my powerful
appendages.
Less intelligent foes
are more
worthwhile opponents
than others
of my species.
Searching for an enemy
prepared to
strike it to
death
looking for the details
that signify
something’s there.

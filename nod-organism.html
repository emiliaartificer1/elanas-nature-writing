Poem 2: Adventurous

We are
going on an
adventure.
Adventuring to many
places
with countless spaces
inside an endless universe.
We
wait.
We
wonder
what these other planets
are like.
We remember being we
in Lante,
in Baltiel,
in Lortisse.
We wait for another
adventure,
ever hungry for more
complexity
to explore.

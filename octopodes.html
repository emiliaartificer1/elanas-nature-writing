Poem 5: Crown and Reach

The Reach of an Octopus is
complex, more so than
a Human,
a Portiid,
a Corvid,
even a Mantis Shrimp.
The Reach does not obey
anyone.
Yet the Crown is the
master, ever trying to
take control.
Reaches calculate,
Crowns wonder,
Octopodes explore.
Exploring the smallest
crevices,
grappling with
their coworkers,
sharing ideas
from Reach to Reach.
Ideas that could
start wars,
create or abolish policies,
build a superluminal
spaceship,
even faster than
light itself.
Those Reaches and Crowns,
knowing and learning and
understanding.
Disra Senkovi created them,
but they grasped
the reins,
asked their creator
unanswerable questions,
grappled with
the universe,
with entropy,
to become themselves,
masters of their planet,
the waterworld Damascus,
teeming with life.

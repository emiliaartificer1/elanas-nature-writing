Poem 1: Fabian's Death

Fabian, you were my coworker my colleague my friend I never meant to kill you I never meant to hurt you but I did and you died. Fabian, why did you do this to me? You’re a martyr, famous now in history. I never hated you, so why did I kill you? Why did you have to die? What did the Messenger even say to you, did she tell you to sacrifice yourself, or was it your plan? Whichever way truly happened, I’ll never forgive myself for you.
